How Patronage Works

Your purchases build
a financial position.

Patronage is not a rebate program. It is a contractual obligation — documented in your HomeForty Membership Agreement before the purchase year begins. It operates in two parts simultaneously.

01
Annual Cash Payment
Within 60 days of fiscal year-end, 50% of your patronage allocation is paid in cash. No discretion. No board vote required on your portion. The obligation is established before you place your first order.
02
Retained Credit Accumulation
The other 50% is logged to your named account in dollar terms — not units, not credits, not points. Dollars. You see the balance grow on your annual statement every February.
03
Exit Conversion Formula
At a triggering event — sale of the company, defined distribution — your retained balance converts to cash using a formula agreed today. Your balance ÷ all member balances × 5% of net proceeds. You can calculate your payout right now with hypothetical numbers.
04
Eight-Year Rotation
Retained balances older than eight years pay out in cash regardless of any triggering event. The program honors its commitments on a rolling basis — you don't have to wait for a company sale to see your retained credits converted.
05
Optional Syndicate Rollover
Each year, you elect what happens to your cash patronage payment. Take it as cash. Hold it. Or redirect it into the current Syndicate SPV — deploying earnings from your seed purchases into agricultural investments you believe in.

The Syndicate

Investment access
built for operators.

The Syndicate is a private investment community for HomeForty members. Agricultural deal flow. Individual SPVs. Opportunities you can evaluate with your own expertise — not a black box managed by people who have never farmed.

Membership Access · HomeForty Members Only

Each investment opportunity in The Syndicate is structured as a separate Special Purpose Vehicle — its own LLC, its own investment, its own cap table. One deal at a time. No blind pools. No obligation to participate in any deal you don't believe in.

You evaluate each opportunity with the same expertise you apply to your own operation. When you invest, you deploy capital alongside other members who share your knowledge of what works on a farm.

And when you hold patronage credits you want to put to work, you can elect to roll them directly into an available SPV. Your seed purchases create the investment capital. The program is designed so value compounds.
